# BASE CROWN

## Overview
BASE CROWN is a fully onchain strategy and progression game built as a Base Mini App.
Players collect Crown Fragments, forge Relics, complete quests, attack rivals, climb leaderboards and compete for rewards.
All game state is stored on Base. No centralized database is required.
The game economy revolves around CROWN, Fragments, Keys and Relics.
Players can play for free, while Genesis Crown Key holders receive additional rewards and daily advantages.
The objective is to grow stronger, forge better Relics, earn higher rankings and compete for seasonal rewards.

## Core Loop
1.Claim daily Spins and Attacks.
2.Use Spins to earn Fragments, Keys, CROWN and other rewards.
3.Use Attacks to steal resources from other players.
4.Complete Quests for additional rewards.
5.Collect Fragments and CROWN.
6.Forge Relics using Fragments and CROWN.
7.Relics provide bonuses and progression advantages.
8.Climb the leaderboard through activity and score.
9.Earn seasonal rewards.
10.Repeat daily.

## Spins
Daily Spins

Normal Players:

- 5 Free Spins per day

Genesis Crown Key Holders:

- 10 Free Spins per day

Daily Spins reset every 24 hours.

Unused Spins do not carry over.

Extra Spins

Players can purchase additional Spins using ETH.

Extra Spins cannot be purchased using CROWN.

Reason:
Prevent players from wasting large amounts of CROWN on luck-based mechanics.

Spin Rewards

Possible Rewards:

- CROWN
- Crown Fragments
- Crown Keys
- Extra Attack
- Extra Spin
- Crown Chest

Reward Probabilities

Common Rewards:

- CROWN
- Fragments

Uncommon Rewards:

- Keys
- Extra Attacks

Rare Rewards:

- Crown Chests

Future updates may introduce:

- Rare Relics
- Event Tickets
- Limited Collectibles

Spin Cooldown

No cooldown between Spins.

Players may use all available Spins immediately.

Spin Security

All reward generation is performed onchain.

Spin results are recorded on Base.

Purpose

Spins provide the primary daily progression loop.

Players use Spins to collect resources required for:

- Relic Forging
- Relic Upgrades
- Event Participation
- Leaderboard Progression

## Attacks
Daily Attacks

Normal Players:

- 5 Free Attacks per day

Genesis Crown Key Holders:

- 7 Free Attacks per day

Daily Attacks reset every 24 hours.

Unused Attacks do not carry over.

Extra Attacks

Players may purchase additional Attacks using:

- CROWN
- ETH

The player chooses which currency to use.

Attack Targets

Players can attack other active players.

Targets are selected from available player profiles.

Attack Rewards

Successful attacks may reward:

- Crown Fragments
- Crown Keys
- Score

CROWN tokens can never be stolen directly.

Relic NFTs can never be stolen.

Genesis Crown Key NFTs can never be stolen.

Attack Risk

Each attack has a success rate.

Success chance depends on:

- Attacker Relics
- Defender Relics
- Active Shields
- Future game mechanics

Shield Protection

Players may activate Shields.

While Shield is active:

- Fragments cannot be stolen
- Keys cannot be stolen

Shield duration may vary depending on the Shield type.

Score System

Successful attacks increase Score.

Failed attacks may grant reduced Score or no Score.

Score determines:

- Leaderboard position
- League ranking
- Seasonal rewards

Battle Results

Attack outcomes are stored onchain.

Every battle generates a permanent game record.

Purpose

Attacks are the primary competitive mechanic.

Players attack rivals to:

- Obtain Fragments
- Obtain Keys
- Increase Score
- Progress faster
- Compete for leaderboard positions

## Fragments
Overview

Crown Fragments are the primary progression resource in Base Crown.

Fragments are more valuable than CROWN for long-term progression because they cannot be purchased directly.

How To Obtain Fragments

Players can earn Fragments through:

- Daily Spins
- Successful Attacks
- Quests
- Chests
- Seasonal Rewards
- Future Events

Main Uses

Fragments are required for:

- Forging Relics
- Upgrading Relics
- Entering Special Events
- Future Crafting Systems

Relic Forge Requirements

Example:

Common Relic:

- 100 Fragments
- 1,000 CROWN

Rare Relic:

- 500 Fragments
- 5,000 CROWN

Epic Relic:

- 2,000 Fragments
- 20,000 CROWN

Legendary Relic:

- 10,000 Fragments
- 100,000 CROWN

Mythic Relic:

- Future Expansion

Attack Exposure

Fragments can be stolen through successful Attacks.

Only unprotected Fragments are vulnerable.

Protected Fragments cannot be stolen.

Fragment Storage

Players store Fragments inside their Crown Vault.

Vault capacity determines how many Fragments can be protected.

Future upgrades may increase Vault capacity.

Transferability

Transferable: No

Tradable: No

Stealable: Yes

Burnable: No

Strategic Importance

Fragments are the core progression resource.

Every major progression system in Base Crown ultimately depends on collecting and spending Fragments.
